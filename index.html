Our Story


We met in a bar.

No, but really. Matt and Amy met one fateful night in August 2012, at a hip new bar in downtown San Francisco. Matt was celebrating his friend Dash's birthday, and Amy was stopping in for a drink with her sister, Kelly, and her sister’s boyfriend (now husband), Enoch.

As the night progressed, Amy turned her attention to the loud boys at the next table. Amy approached Matt, and the two quickly bonded over a mutual love of burritos, bloody marys, and San Francisco’s Mission district.

Before they knew it, they had chatted the night away and their friends were gone. Five years and two coasts later, the pair is tying the knot and starting the next phase of their lives together.
